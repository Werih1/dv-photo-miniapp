DV Form Guide

The photo must be a JPEG file with exactly 600x600 pixels. The head height must be between 50% and 69% of the total image height (300-414 pixels).

Eyes should be positioned between 56% and 69% from the bottom of the image. The background must be uniform white or light gray with no shadows or patterns.

The photo must be recent (taken within the last 6 months) and show your full face looking directly at the camera with a neutral expression.

Adjust Guide Lines

Instructions:
  • Red line: Top of head
  • Blue line: Bottom of chin
  • Green line: Eye center (dashed)
  • Brown line: Vertical center (dashed)
